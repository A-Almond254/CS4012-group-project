Here is a guide to all the must see things in the wonderful country of Japan!

Mount Fuji

This extremely recognisable landmark is a must see on any

trip to Japan! The enormous mountain is known for its

beautiful snow capped peak that can be seen from Tokyo.

It's a sight you'll want to see with your own eyes.

Tokyo tower

This titan of a structure located in the capital city is an

amazing sight to see! It's something you won't want to miss

out on seeing while your in the area!

Tokyo skytree

This gigantic broadcast tower is up there as one of the tallest

manmade structures in the world. That alone makes it something

you should see for yourself. There is also an observation deck

open to the public, which allows you to see a remarkable view!
